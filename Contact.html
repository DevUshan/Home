Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our process

How long does a typical web project take?
The timeline varies based on project complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your specific requirements.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
Yes, we offer various maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular backups, security monitoring, and content updates.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates, depending on the scope of work. We provide transparent quotes after understanding your project requirements. Our basic websites start at $499, with custom solutions priced accordingly.
